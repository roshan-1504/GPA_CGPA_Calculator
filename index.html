Are you tired of losing your progress when using online GPA calculators?

Look no further!

These calculators ensure that your values are preserved even after the page is refreshed.

GPA Calculator

  • Enter Number of courses in the semester & click 'Create'.
  • Input the credits and grades of your courses
  • Click on Calculate GPA to get your GPA
  • Never worry about accidentally refreshing the page - your data will stay intact!

CGPA Calculator

  • Enter the number of semesters & click 'Create'.
  • Give the total credits and GPA obtained in that semester
  • Click on Calculate CGPA to get your CGPA
  • Never worry about accidentally refreshing the page - your data will stay intact!
